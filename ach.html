ACHIEVEMENTS
🙏



Achievements are a way to reward and encourage players in video games. Xbox 360 was the first console to have a system setup for achievements, and soon after the Playstation 3 implemented trophies. Achievements, depending on their difficulty, will award you “gamerscore”. This gamerscore is a measure of skill to some, and is a reason to strive for all achievements.



What you are holding in your hands is the outcome of Ocean Albin's bachelor thesis Playing with ℜealities, written and designed while studying in the 4th year at the Graphic Design department of the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.
